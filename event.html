#2 Integrity and Complience Tas Force Seminar

The IFRS Foundation has announced the creation of its new International Sustainability Standards Board (ISSB) that will develop a comprehensive global baseline of high-quality sustainability disclosure standards to meet investors' information needs.

As the member of Integrity and Compliance Task Force B20 Indonesia, IAI in collaboration with the B20, IFAC, and the ISSB is organizing a webinar on Global Baseline for Business and Investors: B20 – IAI – IFAC – ISSB Outreach on June 22, 2022 at 2.00-4.30 PM Jakarta.

The discussion will provide the opportunity for the business community and stakeholders to engage with ISSB and its upcoming standard. To encourage business community and other stakeholders to provide inputs on the exposure drafts IFRS Sustainability Disclosure Standard.
