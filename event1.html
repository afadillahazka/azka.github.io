#2 Integrity and Complience Task Force Seminar

Topic Global Baseline for Business and Investors: B20 – IAI – IFAC – ISSB Outreach
Description As member of Task Force Integrity and Compliance B20, IAI in collaboration with the B20, IFAC, and the ISSB is organizing a webinar on Global Baseline for Business and Investors: B20 – IAI – IFAC – ISSB Outreach. We are honoured to have the B20 full support to recognize this important discussion as part of the B20 Side Event.

The discussion will provide the opportunity for the business community and stakeholders to engage with ISSB and its upcoming standard. To encourage business community and other stakeholders to provide inputs on the exposure drafts IFRS Sustainability Disclosure Standard.
